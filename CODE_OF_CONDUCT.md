# Code of Conduct

We adopt the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (v2.1) to foster an open and welcoming community.

Our Pledge
- Create a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio‑economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language; be respectful of differing viewpoints.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully; focus on what is best for the community.
- Avoid harassment, trolling, and personal or political attacks.

Enforcement
- Report incidents privately to: security@nabi.example (replace with your contact).
- The Moderator group will review and determine appropriate actions, which may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
